A colour scheme born from Johannesburg after dark — the quiet blue-grey of a storm rolling over the Highveld, lit up by the electric energy of the city that never sleeps.

Nights is the dark variant. Tokyo Night Storm provides the muted environment — editor chrome, panels, gutters — while syntax highlighting pulls from the synthwave palette: hot pinks, neon greens, electric purples.

Morning is the light variant. Same energy, softer backdrop. The storm has passed, the city wakes up.

Two modes. One palette. Multiple apps.
